"Sometimes When I Dream"

Her and I were strangers who happened to share an abusive ex, so as I went looking in the dark for answers - she appeared like a dream. As a way to heal we went to the beach to bond by filming two seperate independent projects, now sometimes when I dream I can still feel the sun burning into me.

This video is a snipet from a longer, short experiential film shot, directed and produced by JR. 2017
